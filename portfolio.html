Boom Entertainment

I joined Boom Entertainment while it was still in it’s start-up phase. I became the sole graphic designer for the company while it grew. I designed and managed graphical and photo content for the company site, free-to-play sports app, and the App Store. I worked closely with the marketing team to create static and motion graphics for social media campaigns, email campaigns, and advertising. I was responsible for the internal company newsletter. I coordinated with developers to build a new templating system for image handling in multiple gaming apps. I also designed the company internal merchandising, distributed to employees.

Fossil Farms

I joined Fossil Farms as the sole Graphics/Art team. I created and ensured upholding of a Visual Brand Identity Guideline. This included creating new logos to be used universally across the company. Document-ing fonts and color ways. This was utilized across the company website, product packaging, infographics, social media campaigns, email campaigns, flyers, online advertising, physical signage, apparel, banners, and a vehicle wrap. I was also responsible for product photography as well as the photo editing for the products being sold on the website and the physical store.

University of Miami

I attended the University of Miami, where I earned my Degrees in Studio Art and Computer Science. While there, I was a member and the Chair of the university’s Cinematic Art’s Commission and Hurricane Production clubs. I had the opportunity to serve as the Graphic Designer for the clubs. I even continued working with the organizations after my graduation in May 2018.

Freelance Projects

I’ve taken on freelance design work for small businesses and events. I partnered with a t-shirt company to redesign their branding and logo identity, helping them modernize their visual presence and better connect with their audience. I also designed and built a fully responsive Squarespace e-commerce site, tailored to the client’s products and user needs. For a large-scale scavenger hunt event, I created a series of themed digital logos that combined playful visuals with cohesive branding. These projects were self-directed, often fast-paced, and involved working closely with clients to define goals, incorporate feedback, and deliver polished final assets.
